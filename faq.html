Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our study guide

This website is a comprehensive study guide designed specifically for linguistics students preparing for their examinations. It provides detailed explanations of key topics across multiple linguistics courses, interactive practice tests, flashcards, and study resources. Our goal is to help students excel in their linguistics studies by providing all the materials they need in one accessible platform.

This website is designed for linguistics students at all levels, particularly those studying courses like Introduction to Linguistics, Phonetics, Phonology, Morphology, Historical Linguistics, and related subjects. Whether you're a beginner just starting your linguistics journey or an advanced student preparing for exams, you'll find valuable resources here. The content covers both 100-level and 200-level courses.

This website is completely free to use! We believe that quality educational resources should be accessible to all students. There are no hidden fees, subscriptions, or premium features. All study materials, practice tests, flashcards, and resources are available at no cost to help you succeed in your studies.

Our website helps with exam preparation in several ways:

  • Comprehensive Study Materials: Detailed explanations of all key topics from your courses
  • Practice Tests: Interactive tests with rotating questions that change each time you take them
  • Progress Tracking: Mark topics as completed to track your study progress
  • Flashcards: Interactive flashcards to help memorize key terms and definitions
  • Visual Resources: Charts, diagrams, and visual aids like the IPA chart
  • Search Function: Quickly find specific topics or keywords
  • Dark Mode: Study comfortably at any time of day

All features are designed to help you understand concepts deeply and prepare effectively for your examinations.

Using our practice tests is simple:

  1. Go to the Practice Test page
  2. Select the course you want to practice (e.g., LIN 101, LIN 103, etc.)
  3. Click "Start Test" to begin
  4. Answer each question by selecting your choice
  5. Use "Previous" and "Next" buttons to navigate
  6. Click "Submit Test" when you're done
  7. Review your results and see explanations for any incorrect answers

Important: Each time you take a test, you'll get different questions! This ensures you get comprehensive practice across all topics. The questions rotate automatically, so you can take the same test multiple times and always see new content.
